GiddyUp!

A Dice Game

Rules:

  • When you click the Take a Gamble! button, you will roll a die, up to 10 times.
  • If you roll a 1, the game is over.
  • 2 or 3 gets you nothing but another roll.
  • Unlucky 4! When you roll a 4, you have to tip your server.
  • 5 wins 5 gold ducats and another roll.
  • 6 wins you 6 gold and another roll.

And remember,
don't let your yearnings get ahead of your earnings.
